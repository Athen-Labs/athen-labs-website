Audit My Model - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2025

1. Data Collection

When you use Audit My Model, we collect information related to the financial models and spreadsheets you upload for analysis. This may include financial data, formulas, and other content contained within your files.

2. Use of Your Data

We use the data you provide solely for the purpose of analyzing your model and providing feedback. Your data is processed using our AI systems to identify potential issues and suggest improvements.

3. Data Retention

We retain your uploaded files only for the time necessary to process your request. Files are automatically deleted from our servers after processing unless you have explicitly opted to save your analysis.
