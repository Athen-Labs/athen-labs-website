Athen Labs, LLC Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2025

1. Introduction

At Athen Labs, LLC, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, products, and services.

2. Information Collection

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when using our services, including but not limited to your name, email address, and usage data. We may also collect non-personal information about how you interact with our services.

3. Account Information

For all our productivity and job search applications (excluding consumer apps), we collect and store account information including login credentials, name, email, and other information necessary to provide our services. This information is securely stored and processed in accordance with industry standards.

4. User Control of Account Information

You have the ability to view, update, or delete your account information at any time through your account settings. If you delete your account, all associated personal information will be removed from our active databases, though some information may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes.

5. Payment Processing

Payment information for subscriptions and purchases is processed through Stripe, a secure third-party payment processor. We do not store your complete credit card information on our servers. All payment processing is conducted in accordance with PCI-DSS standards. For more information about how Stripe handles your payment data, please refer to Stripe's Privacy Policy.

6. Use of Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, to communicate with you, and to comply with legal obligations. We may also use anonymized data for research and analytics purposes.

7. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with service providers who assist us in operating our business, as required by law, or with your consent.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Your continued use of our services after any such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@athenlabs.com.

Audit My Model - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2025

1. Data Collection

When you use Audit My Model, we collect and process information from the files you upload for analysis. This includes extracting object models and content from your financial models and spreadsheets to apply our AI analysis.

2. Data Storage and Processing

Your uploaded files are processed on our servers and the extracted information is stored to provide you with analysis history. While we do not examine your files manually, the information is transmitted to our servers and processed by our AI systems.

3. Account Information

We collect and store your account information including login credentials, name, email, and billing details. This information is necessary to provide you with access to our services and maintain your analysis history.

4. Data Retention

We retain the extracted information and analysis results in your account history to allow you to review past analyses. You have the ability to delete this history at any time through your account settings.

5. User Responsibilities

You are responsible for ensuring that files you upload do not contain sensitive information. We recommend obfuscating sensitive data before uploading files - this is an option we remind you about but you must take this action yourself before uploading.

6. Security Measures

While we implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, we are not responsible for breaches that may occur. We encourage users to exercise caution when uploading any business documents and to obfuscate sensitive information.

Quick Note AI - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2025

1. Audio Recording

Quick Note AI allows you to record audio through your device's microphone for meetings, lectures, and other events. You control when to start and stop recording, and all recordings are initially stored locally on your device.

2. Transcription Processing

When you choose to process your recordings, the audio is sent to our AI providers for transcription and analysis. These providers process the audio to generate text transcripts and may also create summaries of the content.

3. Data Storage

Your original audio recordings are not stored on our servers. However, the transcripts generated from your recordings are processed by our AI providers, who may maintain a history of these processed files in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. User Access and Control

You can access your history of transcriptions through the app. You have the ability to delete notes from your device at any time. While this removes the content from your device, please be aware that processed transcripts may remain in the AI providers' systems according to their retention policies.

5. Third-Party Processing

We rely on third-party AI providers to process your audio recordings for transcription. These providers have their own privacy policies regarding how they handle and retain the data they process.

6. Payment Processing

Quick Note AI is available on iOS devices. All payments and subscriptions are processed through the Apple App Store. We do not collect, process, or store your payment information. Subscription management, including cancellations and refunds, must be done through your Apple App Store account settings.

7. No Account Creation

Quick Note AI does not require you to create an account. All app data is stored locally on your device. If you wish to stop using the service, you can simply delete the app from your device. No additional termination process is required as we do not maintain user accounts or store your data on our servers.

8. Legal Compliance

It is your responsibility to ensure you have the legal right to record conversations, meetings, or lectures in your jurisdiction. Many regions require consent from all parties being recorded. We recommend familiarizing yourself with local laws regarding audio recording before using this feature.

9. AI Limitations

Our AI systems may make mistakes in transcriptions or summaries. The accuracy of transcription depends on various factors, including audio quality, background noise, speaker clarity, and technical limitations of AI systems.

Ace My Classes - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 25, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: We use Firebase Authentication to manage your account. This includes an anonymous User ID, which can be linked to your Apple ID if you choose to sign in. When you sign in with Apple, we may receive your name and email address to personalize your experience.
  • Your Content: We collect and store the data you create to provide the service, including courses, notes, chapters, schedules, deliverables, subtasks, work sessions, flashcards, review history, audio recordings from lectures or voice quick-add, drawings, images, video timelapses from the Whiteboard feature, and AI Chat conversations.
  • Analytics: The app does not currently collect analytics data on user behavior. This may change in the future to help us improve the app, in which case this policy will be updated.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide the Service: Your data powers the app's features, such as organizing notes, scheduling reminders, and syncing your data.
  • For AI Features: To provide AI-powered features, certain data is sent to our AI service provider (Google), including audio recordings for transcription, summarization, and content generation; text from your notes and recordings to provide context for AI Chat; and whiteboard data (audio, video, images) for AI-powered evaluation.
  • Service Improvement: We may use anonymized data in the future to understand usage patterns and improve our services.

3. Data Storage and Sharing

  • Firebase & Google Cloud: All of Your Content is stored securely on Google Cloud Platform using Firebase services (Firestore and Cloud Storage). Your data is protected by security rules that prevent other users from accessing it.
  • Google Generative AI: Content is sent to Google's AI services for processing. According to Google's policies, this data is used to provide the feature and is not used to train their models without explicit consent. Audio files uploaded for processing are automatically deleted once the AI job is complete.
  • We Do Not Sell Your Data: We will never sell your personal information or Your Content to third parties.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including using secure cloud infrastructure and implementing strict access control rules. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • We retain Your Content as long as your account is active.
  • You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen. This permanently deletes your user account and all associated data from our systems, including from Firestore and Cloud Storage.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, and delete your data through the features provided in the app. For complete data deletion, use the "Delete Account" feature.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. We will notify you of any significant changes by displaying a notice within the app or by other means.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at hello@athenlabs.com.

Ace SAT - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2025

1. Local Data Processing

Ace SAT is a basic application that operates primarily on your device. All core educational data, including your answers to questions and progress tracking, is stored locally on your device.

2. Usage Data Collection

We collect anonymous usage data through Mixpanel analytics to help improve your experience with the app. This includes tracking how you interact with features and content to help us understand what's most valuable and identify areas for improvement. Your personal information is never linked to this usage data, and we do not track sensitive information like your specific answers to test questions.

3. Score Prediction

The app includes a score prediction feature that uses a standard algorithm (not AI) to estimate potential SAT scores based on your performance within the app. This calculation happens entirely on your device without external processing.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use basic third-party services provided by your device's operating system or app store for functions like app installation and updates. These interactions are subject to the privacy policies of those platform providers but do not involve sharing your educational data.

5. Data Control

Since all your educational data is stored locally on your device, you can delete this information by either clearing the app's data through your device settings or by uninstalling the application.

6. Payment Processing

Ace SAT is available on both iOS and Android devices:

  • For iOS users: All payments and subscriptions are processed through the Apple App Store. We do not collect, process, or store your payment information. Subscription management, including cancellations and refunds, must be done through your Apple App Store account settings.
  • For Android users: All payments and subscriptions are processed through the Google Play Store. We do not collect, process, or store your payment information. Subscription management, including cancellations and refunds, must be done through your Google Play Store account settings.

7. No Account Creation

Ace SAT does not require you to create an account. All app data is stored locally on your device. If you wish to stop using the service, you can simply delete the app from your device. No additional termination process is required as we do not maintain user accounts or store your data on our servers.
