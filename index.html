Helping people through broken systems

Athen Labs is developing AI that helps people through complex systems.

We are researching and building tools for the moments when digital systems are confusing, inaccessible, or simply too hard to use, and for the organizations trying to make those systems better.

What We're Developing Why Now

What We're Developing

Two focused systems we are actively researching and building around the same belief: people should not be blocked by the systems they depend on.

Northstar

A real-time accessibility agent we are developing to help users get through broken pages, confusing forms, and dead-end navigation when the interface itself is the problem.

AutoWCAG

An AI remediation pipeline we are developing for inaccessible PDFs and web content, designed to extract structure, generate semantics, repair documents, and validate output.

Why Now

Accessibility is still unsolved, and when systems fail, real people are the ones left dealing with the friction.

94.8%

of the top million home pages still had detectable WCAG failures in 2025.

Apr 24

DOJ accessibility deadlines begin hitting larger public entities on April 24, 2026.

2-sided

The same failures that block accessibility also block AI agents, which makes repair and navigation part of the same problem.

Technology should feel more usable, and more humane.

Athen Labs is an early-stage effort focused on researching and building better ways to help people through broken systems while giving institutions practical ways to improve what sits underneath.
